FAQS

On the class page, click the section you want to add students. And in the right upper right button, click on “Add Student.” As you notice there are three options on how you can add students, create student account, join class code, and import students. You can use the following depending on your needs. You can also add student as many as you want.

To be able to send bonus, fines, bills, and funds, just click on a specific student you want to transact with and enter the amount you want them to receive. You can send funds and bonus to different students at the same time. You can also customize the title of the fund if it is not on the default options.

To bid in an auction item, click on your chosen item and enter the amount you want to bid. If you are the top bidder, your name will be shown at the lower part of the item and the amount you bid.

On the jobs menu, you can apply to your chosen job. You have to write a description that will describe your strength and why you are deserving to get the job. The President will be the one incharge of accepting and rejecting applications of the applicants.

Each student can receive their salary at the end of the month. You can check claim your salary on the dashboard.
